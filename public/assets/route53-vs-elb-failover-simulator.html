Comparing Route 53 DNS Failover vs. Elastic Load Balancer (ELB) Failover

Route 53 (DNS Failover)

DNS failover relies on health checks and TTL. Traffic continues to the dead instance until the client's cached TTL expires and it fetches the new IP.

Dropped/Failed: 0

Elastic Load Balancer

The ELB actively proxies traffic. The moment a backend node fails a health check, traffic is instantly re-routed to healthy nodes with zero client-side delay.

Dropped/Failed: 0
Proxy Status: Instant Routing
